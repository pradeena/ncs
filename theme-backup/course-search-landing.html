About BCA

BCA is an undergraduate course that encompasses a curriculum based on concepts and applications of computers.  The full form of BCA is "Bachelors of computer application" Usually, the course duration for this particular course is 3 years. Students have good prospects after pursuing BCA. They can pursue MCA or MBA after completion of the course.

This course is particularly for the students who wants to study computers and would further want to join software companies in their future.
